Our Facilities

At Beachview Guest House, we strive to provide our guests with the best experience possible. Below are the facilities we offer to make your stay enjoyable and comfortable.

Free Wi-Fi

Stay connected with our high-speed internet, available throughout the guest house.

Breakfast Service

Start your day with a delicious breakfast prepared with local ingredients, served every morning in our dining area.

Sea View Rooms

Enjoy stunning views of the Dornoch coastline from the comfort of your room. The perfect way to unwind.

Private Parking

We offer private parking for all of our guests, so you can enjoy your stay without worrying about your vehicle.

Pet-Friendly

We welcome your furry friends! Bring your pets along and enjoy a vacation together.

Fitness Center

Stay in shape during your stay with access to our fully equipped fitness center.

24/7 Reception

Our friendly staff is available around the clock to assist with anything you need during your stay.
