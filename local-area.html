Explore the Local Area

Beachview Guest House is located in the beautiful town of Dornoch, Scotland, known for its stunning beaches, historic sites, and charming town center. Here are some attractions you can explore during your stay:

Dornoch Beach

A beautiful sandy beach perfect for walks, picnics, and enjoying the scenic views of the coastline.

Dornoch Cathedral

One of the oldest cathedrals in Scotland, offering a glimpse into the rich history and architecture of the region.

Royal Dornoch Golf Club

Known as one of the best golf courses in the world, this club offers a challenge for golfers of all skill levels.
